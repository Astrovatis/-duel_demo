📜 DUEL RULES

Basic Rules:

  • You select K numbers from 1 to 50.
  • 12 numbers are drawn each round.
  • In case of a tie on top, a 13th number (Tie-Breaker) is drawn.
  • The player with the most hits wins.
  • The pot is shared in case the tie remains after the 13th number.

Payout Structure:

  • Normally one player wins the pot (except if a tie remains).
  • Absolute (K hits): Stake × Multiplier + Pot share.
  • Absolute-1 (K-1 hits): Pot share + Extra 50% of the total pot from the provider.
  • 13th number (Tie-Breaker): Decides the winner among the tied players.

Absolute Multipliers:

  • 6 numbers: 500×
  • 7 numbers: 1.500×
  • 8 numbers: 5.000×
  • 9 numbers: 12.000×
  • 10 numbers: 100.000×
  • 11 numbers: 500.000×
  • 12 numbers: 1.000.000× (for 50€ stake: 20.000.000×)

Timings:

  • 60"-46": Display of previous draw and results (you can clear them using the "Clear" button).
  • 45"-11": Number selection for the next round.
  • 10"-0": Selections lock.
  • 0": Draw is performed.

Table Types:

  • Open: The countdown starts immediately when the table is created.
  • Closed: A join request must be accepted by the table owner.
  • Closed: The initial countdown starts once all seats are filled.
  • Any empty seats at 10" are filled by bots.

Surrender:

  • Before selections are locked: You only lose the stake of the current round.
  • After selections are locked: You participate in the current draw but lose the stake of the next round as well.
