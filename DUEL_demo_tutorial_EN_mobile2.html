    📜 DUEL RULES

    Basic Rules:

    • Pick K numbers from 1 to 50
    • Numbers are drawn 12 numbers per round
    • In case of a tie, a 13th number (Tie-Breaker)
    • The player with the most hits wins
    • The pot is shared in case of a tie
    • In case of a tie, the 13th number (tie-breaker) decides

    Winnings Distribution:

    • One winner the pot (except ties)
    • Absolute (K hits): Stake × Multiplier + Pot share
    • Absolute-1 (K-1 hits): Pot share + 50% pot from provider
    • Tie: The pot is shared
    • 13th number: In a tie, the winner is decided by

    Absolute Multipliers:

    • 6 numbers: 500×
    • 7 numbers: 1.500×
    • 8 numbers: 5.000×
    • 9 numbers: 12.000×
    • 10 numbers: 100.000×
    • 11 numbers: 500.000×
    • 12 numbers: 1.000.000× (50€ stake: 20.000.000×)

    Timing:

    • 60"-46": Show previous draw and results (clears with the Clear button)
    • 45"-11": Pick numbers for the next round
    • 10"-0": Lock-in
    • 0": Draw

    Table Types:

    • Open: The timer starts immediately when the table is created
    • Closed: Join request requires approval
    • Closed: Start time counts from the moment the last player joins
    • Any empty seats at 10" are filled with bots

    Surrender:

    • Before lock-in: You only lose the stake of the current round
    • After lock-in: You participate in the current draw and lose the stake for the next round
